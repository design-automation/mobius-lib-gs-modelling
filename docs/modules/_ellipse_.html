ArcFromPlane

  • ArcFromPlane(plane: IPlane, radius_x: number, radius_y: number, angles: [number, number]): IEllipse
    • Defined in ellipse.ts:120

    Adds a circular arc to the model based on a plane and radius

    Arc will be constructed parallel to the plane with the origin of the plane as the center point of the ellipse that forms the arc
    Arc will be constructed starting from the x-axis of the specified plane and follows the ellipse in the specified direction until it reaches the angle specified

    Parameters

    • plane: IPlane

      Plane to construct arc on

    • radius_x: number

      Radius x of ellipse

    • radius_y: number

      Radius y of ellipse

    • angles: [number, number]

      Two angles between 0 and 360, for start and end of arc.

    Returns IEllipse

    New arc (ellipse) if successful
